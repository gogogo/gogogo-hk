The Gogogo Project aims to provide a comprehensive and easy-to-use transit planning service for Hong Kong. We want Gogogo to be the service you can rely on whenever you want to find out how to get from point A to point B within Hong Kong.

The Gogogo Project is currently building the service and maintaining the transit database with team members collaborating using open source methods. If you are interested join us! You may participate in our gogogo-hk-discuss google group, or contact Ben directly (his contact is available in gogogo-hk-discuss). Apart from software developers, we will also need contributors for our the community-maintained transit database.
